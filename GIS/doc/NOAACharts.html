NOAA Raster Nautical Charts

Georefrenced raster images of NOAA nautical charts.

Source: http://www.charts.noaa.gov/InteractiveCatalog/nrnc.shtml

Raster File Processing

Transcribed from BSB format to GeoTiff and reprojected to EPSG:4326 Lat/Lon coordinate system using:

gdalwarp -t_srs EPSG:4326 -of Gtiff [chart#].KAP [chart#].tif
